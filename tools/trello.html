What is Trello?

Trello is a visual project management tool built around boards, lists, and cards. It’s perfect for freelancers who want a simple, drag‑and‑drop workflow to manage tasks, clients, and ongoing projects.

Key Features

  • Kanban-style boards
  • Drag‑and‑drop task management
  • Team collaboration
  • Automations with Butler
  • Integrations with Slack, Google Drive, Notion, and more

Free plan available. Paid plans start at $5/month.
