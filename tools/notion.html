Overview

Notion is a flexible workspace that lets freelancers manage notes, tasks, databases, and projects in one place.

Best For

  • Freelancers managing multiple clients
  • Content planning and documentation
  • Personal knowledge management

Pros

  • Highly customizable
  • Great for documentation and wikis
  • Powerful database features

Cons

  • Can feel overwhelming at first
  • Offline support is limited

Pricing

Free plan available. Paid plans add collaboration, permissions, and more storage.
