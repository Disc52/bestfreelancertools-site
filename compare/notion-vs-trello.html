Notion vs Trello

Which tool is better for freelancers?

Quick Summary

Notion is better for documentation and complex workflows. Trello is better for simple, visual task boards.

When to choose Notion

  • You want one workspace for notes, tasks, and databases
  • You like building custom systems

When to choose Trello

  • You want a simple Kanban board
  • You prefer visual task management
