What is Canva?

Canva is a user‑friendly design platform that helps freelancers create graphics, social media posts, presentations, and marketing materials without needing advanced design skills.

Key Features

  • Thousands of templates
  • Drag‑and‑drop editor
  • Brand kits and color palettes
  • AI tools like Magic Design
  • Export in PNG, JPG, PDF, MP4, and more

Pricing

Free plan available. Canva Pro starts at $12.99/month.
