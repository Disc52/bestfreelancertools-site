What is Figma?

Figma is a collaborative design platform used for UI/UX design, prototyping, and creative work. Freelancers use it to design websites, apps, and brand assets with real‑time collaboration.

Key Features

  • Vector editing and prototyping
  • Design systems and components
  • Plugins and integrations
  • Version history and comments
