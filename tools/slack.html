Slack

Communication platform for teams, clients, and collaboration.

What is Slack?

Slack is a communication platform that helps freelancers and teams collaborate through organized channels, direct messages, file sharing, and integrations with hundreds of apps.

Key Features

  • Channels for organized communication
  • Direct messaging and group chats
  • Voice and video huddles
  • Integrations with Google Drive, Notion, ClickUp, and more
  • Powerful search and file sharing

Pricing

Free plan available. Paid plans start at $7.25/month.
