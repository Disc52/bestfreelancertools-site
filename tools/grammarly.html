What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is an AI‑powered writing assistant that helps freelancers improve grammar, clarity, tone, and overall writing quality. It works across emails, documents, social media, and browser‑based tools.

Key Features

  • Grammar and spelling correction
  • Clarity and tone suggestions
  • AI rewriting and content improvement
  • Plagiarism detection (Premium)
  • Works across browsers, apps, and devices

Pricing

Free plan available. Premium starts at $12/month.
