Project management built for teams and solo freelancers.

What is Asana?

Asana is a project management platform designed to help individuals and teams organize work, track progress, and collaborate efficiently. Freelancers use it to manage client projects and deadlines with clarity.

Key Features

  • Task lists and project boards
  • Timeline and calendar views
  • Workflow automation
  • Team collaboration and messaging
  • Integrations with Slack, Google Drive, and more

Pricing

Free plan available. Premium starts at $10.99/month.
