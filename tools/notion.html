Notion

All‑in‑one workspace for notes, tasks, docs, and project management.

What is Notion?

Notion is a powerful all‑in‑one workspace that combines notes, documents, tasks, databases, and project management into a single flexible platform. Freelancers use it to organize clients, track projects, build wikis, and manage their entire workflow in one place.

Key Features

  • Notes, docs, and rich text editor
  • Databases for tasks, clients, and projects
  • Templates for productivity and planning
  • Team collaboration and comments
  • Integrations with Slack, Google Drive, and more

Pricing

Free plan available. Paid plans start at $8/month.
