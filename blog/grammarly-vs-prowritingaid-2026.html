Grammarly vs ProWritingAid in 2026

Two powerful writing assistants. Which one helps you deliver your best client work?

Grammarly: best for speed and simplicity

Grammarly focuses on fast, real‑time suggestions for grammar, spelling, clarity, and tone. It’s ideal if you write emails, proposals, blog posts, and social content and want quick, low‑friction improvements.

Explore Grammarly →

ProWritingAid: best for deep editing and style

ProWritingAid offers more in‑depth reports on style, readability, repetition, and structure. It’s a great fit if you write long‑form content, books, or detailed articles and want to refine your voice over time.

Which should freelancers choose?

  • Choose Grammarly if you want fast, real‑time checks across everyday writing.
  • Choose ProWritingAid if you care about deep analysis and long‑form editing.
