What is Airtable?

Airtable is a flexible database platform that combines the simplicity of a spreadsheet with the power of a relational database. Freelancers use it to organize clients, projects, content calendars, and more.

Key Features

  • Customizable tables and fields
  • Views: Grid, Kanban, Calendar, Gallery
  • Automations and integrations
  • Collaboration and sharing
  • Templates for content, CRM, and project tracking

Pricing

Free plan available. Paid plans start at $10/month.
